Measure the employability and career mobility of your students with real-time data

In the digital age, the true value of an educational or corporate institution is no longer measured only by the number of graduates or the perceived quality of its programs. Today, the most powerful metric is the real impact your training has on people’s professional lives.

With POK Proof of Knowledge, you don’t just issue verifiable NFT digital credentials: you turn every certificate into a living device for employability and career mobility analysis.

This means you can know, with precision and in real time:

  • Where your graduates are working.
  • If they have changed jobs or industries.
  • How long it takes them to get promoted.
  • What additional certifications they have earned.
  • How their professional network evolves after your program.

How it works in practice

When your institution issues an NFT credential with POK, that credential is linked to the student’s or employee’s professional profile.
If the graduate shares it on LinkedIn or other professional platforms:

  • POK detects changes in position, company, and location.
  • It analyzes patterns by career, program, cohort, or graduation year.
  • It delivers visual, exportable reports that show the real impact of your program, both at the individual and group level.

The information is updated automatically, without relying on manual surveys or alumni calls that often go unanswered.

Tangible benefits for your institution

  1. Reputation and differentiation
    Showing real employability figures positions your institution as a benchmark for quality and results. It’s not just about claiming your programs are good: you can prove it with verifiable data.
  2. Evidence-based academic optimization
    Adapt your curricula based on the skills the market is rewarding. If you see that graduates with a certain profile secure more opportunities, you can reinforce that area in your academic offer.
  3. Post-certification engagement
    By maintaining an active link with your alumni through their credentials, you create a living community that continues to interact with your brand even years after graduation.
  4. Attracting new students and corporate clients
    Employability data is a powerful sales argument both for prospective students and for companies looking to train their employees.
  5. Marketing with real data
    Turn every report into a data-driven marketing campaign, boosting your conversion and credibility.

Conclusion

With POK, employability measurement is no longer a costly, slow, and perception-based process.
Now you can prove, in real time and with verifiable data, that your institution not only educates but also transforms lives and careers.
